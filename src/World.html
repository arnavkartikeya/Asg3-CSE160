3d world with a snake, and a randomly generated forest

The game is simple, the snake will spawn randomly and you have to find the snake and travel towards it. Once you do, the timer stops.

You can reset the snake location and terrain generation by pressing the reset button.

If trees are getting in your way, you can move around them, or left click on the base of the tree to cut a block.

The tree will shrink, and once it dissapears you are able to walk through that space. To grow a tree, right click on the base of an existing tree to increase its height.

I would say the WOW factor would be the random generation of the terrain, with collision and the ability to remove and grow trees.
